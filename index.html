Why go Vegan?

Benefit

Below are some benefits you can get from going vegan:

  • Health: lower cancer rate
  • Care for animals: cultivate kindness
  • Save the world: the emission from cows is strengthening the global warming

    • SDG Allignment

    Having a plant-based diet can also help fulfill sustainable development goals by United Nations

  • SDG 3: good health and well-being
  • SDG 13: climate action
  • SDG 14: life below water
  • SDG 15: life on land
